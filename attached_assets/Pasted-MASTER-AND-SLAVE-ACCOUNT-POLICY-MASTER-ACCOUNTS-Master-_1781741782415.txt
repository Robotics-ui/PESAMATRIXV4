MASTER AND SLAVE ACCOUNT POLICY

MASTER ACCOUNTS

Master accounts are signal providers.

Because they affect many subscribers, they must be reviewed and approved by an administrator before becoming active.

Workflow

User adds Master Account

↓

Status = PENDING_APPROVAL

↓

Account details saved to database

↓

No MetaApi deployment

↓

No CopyFactory strategy creation

↓

No subscribers allowed

↓

Admin reviews account

↓

APPROVED or REJECTED

If APPROVED:

↓

Deploy MetaApi account

↓

Create CopyFactory strategy

↓

Status = ACTIVE

↓

Can receive subscribers

↓

Can send copy trades

If REJECTED:

↓

Status = REJECTED

↓

User receives reason

---

SLAVE ACCOUNTS

Slave accounts are followers.

They do not affect other users.

Therefore they do NOT require admin approval.

Workflow

User adds Slave Account

↓

Create MetaApi account immediately

↓

Deploy automatically

↓

Create CopyFactory Subscriber automatically

↓

Status = ACTIVE

↓

Ready to receive copied trades

---

SUBSCRIPTION ENFORCEMENT

A slave account being ACTIVE does NOT mean it can receive trades.

The system must check subscription status.

Rule

ACTIVE SUBSCRIPTION

↓

Subscriber remains bound

↓

Receives copy trades

EXPIRED SUBSCRIPTION

↓

Subscriber automatically unbound

↓

Receives no copy trades

---

IMPORTANT SEPARATION

Master Account Approval

Controls WHO can provide signals.

Subscription Enforcement

Controls WHO can receive signals.

These are separate systems.

---

Example

User A submits a master account.

Status = PENDING_APPROVAL

Cannot create strategy.

Cannot send trades.

Admin approves.

Status = ACTIVE

Strategy created.

Subscribers can now follow.

---

User B adds a slave account.

Account deploys immediately.

Subscriber account created immediately.

But User B has not paid.

Result:

Slave account exists.

Subscriber account exists.

No strategy binding.

No copied trades received.

After payment:

Subscriber bound to selected strategy.

Copy trades begin.

After subscription expires:

Subscriber automatically unbound.

Copy trades stop immediately.

---

FINAL RULES

Master Accounts:
Require admin approval before deployment.

Slave Accounts:
Deploy automatically.

Only active paid subscriptions may receive copied trades.

Unpaid or expired subscriptions must never receive copied trades even if the slave account is connected and active.
